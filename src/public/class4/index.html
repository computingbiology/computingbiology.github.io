Getting Email Notifications

To get email notifications when there are posts in the Discussions site, you should select the “Watch” menu (upper right on https://github.com/computingbiology/fall2022) and select the notification you want. We recommend at least selecting from the “Custom” menu to get notifications for “Discussions” (if you want notifications for any updates, just select “All Activity”).
